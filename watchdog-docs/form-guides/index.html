Watchdog Form Guides

Watchdog provides all the details & statistics, as well as comments and tips from the Watchdog experts you need to make the most of your form analysis. However, the old printable form guides are still available from the FastTrack website and can be downloaded for each track, including:

  • - the Snapshot provides just that - a snapshot of each race for a Race meet, with recommended bets and form comments for the dogs selected by the form analysts to be the top 4 dogs with the most chance in the race.
  • - the Whisper provides basic form information about each of the dogs in the race, as well as expert comments about each runner in the race.
  • - the Runaway Train provides basic form information for each of the runners in the race. It also provides a speed map of the 1st split speed for each of the dogs.
  • - the Brainstormer includes a cover page providing detailed track statistics. It also shows more detailed form information for each of the dogs in the race, a speed map of 1st split speeds, and some basic infomation about teh overall career statistics and box records.
  • - the most detailed of the downloadable guides, the Brain Stormer also includes a cover page with track statistics. It shows detailed form information for each runner, including it's last 8 races, overall career statistics and box history, and a speed map.

All information displayed in these form guides (and so much more) is available in the app… for every Race… at every Victorian track.

Form Guides Explained

For a breakdown of each of the current form guides and what they contain, this section of our help guide should help you out.

The Snapshot It provides basic information about the race number, start time and distance, and lists the dogs assessed by the Watchdog experts to be the top four dogs for the race, along with a brief comment as to why each one may be worth a look. It also gives recommendations for bets for each race. It does not include any track related information such as track records or form details for any of the runners.

The Whisper provides a brief overview and summary for all races in the meet all on the one page, including places for the previous 5 races for each dog, best time over this distance at this track, the trainer and the starting price. Each race also has a Race Overiew provided by The Watchdog's expert form analysts. Unlike the other downloadable guides, it does not provide any recommended tips for the race meet.

The Runaway Train provides basic form information for each runner in the race, including places for the previous 5 races for each dog, and a summary for the overall history of each dog. Each race has a Race Overiew provided by The Watchdog's expert form analysts, and also provides recommended tips for each race.

The Brainstormer includes a cover page providing detailed track statistics. It also shows basic form information and history for each of the dogs in the race, a speed map of 1st split speeds, and some basic infomation about the overall career statistics and winning box records. On the cover page, it also conains the Watchdog's tips of the day for the race meet.

At the top of each race guide, it shows the race overview, the speedmap and recommended tips for the race.

It contains abbreviated form information for each dog in the race, including details of its last 4 runs. It also contains winning box history for each dog.

The Rocket Scientist is the most detailed of the downloadable guides. It shows detailed form information for each runner, including it's last 8 races, overall career statistics and box history, and a speed map of all runners in each race. Each race has a race overiew provided by The Watchdog's expert form analysts and also provides recommended tips for each race. Like the Brainstomer, it also includes a cover page with track statistics, as well as a recommended Quaddie for the race meet.

At the top of each race guide, it shows the race overview, the speedmap and recommended tips for the race.

It contains the most detailed form information for each dog in the race, including details of its last 8 runs and top three wins not included in the most recent races. It also contains box history for each dog, including places run from each box for every race.
