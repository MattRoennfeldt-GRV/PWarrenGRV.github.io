Watchdog - the ultimate form guide to greyhound racing in Victoria.

Watchdog is the ultimate form guide and research tool for all Victorian greyhound races. It allows you to view the full fields and form analysis for all greyhound races in Victoria, watch live races and special event coverage, view race replays, save your favourite Victorian dogs and trainers and so much more!

Watchdog is developed by Greyhound Racing Victoria (GRV), with expert tips and analysis of all Victorian greyhound races provided by some of the leading experts in the field of greyhound racing. Watchdog is available as an App on your smartphone or view the desktop version in your browser. It is an essential tool for all greyhound racing fans, and it’s free.

Watchdog Key Features:

  • Watch every Victorian greyhound race live and free!
  • Speed maps for all greyhounds
  • Fixed Odds market mover icon
  • Quick form for every runner
  • Full form history and replays for all runners
  • Search for your favourite dogs and trainers
  • Bookmark your favourites in your personal Blackbook
  • Daily email reminders when your favourites are scheduled to run
  • Push Notifications on your phone and Smart Watch so you never miss your favourite Runners again!
  • Integrated live TAB odds and click-to-bet functionality
  • Access to race replays and results for all Victorian greyhound races
  • Race tips, expert analysis and suggested bets
  • Latest greyhound racing news and feature race previews
  • Race calendar of upcoming meetings and results
