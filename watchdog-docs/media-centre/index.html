It's Greyhound Racing News like you've never seen it before!

The Watchdog Media Centre allows you to read, watch and listen to all the latest news and media content about greyhound racing in Victoria, all in one easy to find location.

Watchdog Media Centre Features:

  • Read all the latest articles from Greyhound Racing Victoria, The Watchdog and the Victorian Greyhound Clubs
  • See the latest racing results at a glance, with quick access to all race replays
  • Watch the latest videos from GRV Extra
  • Listen to all the latest audio podcasts from RSN and SEN Track

Make the most of the Watchdog Media Centre today to catch all the early morning segments as The Watchdog provides the best tips on the Bensley show on SEN Track most mornings!

Other Key Features:

  • Watch every Victorian greyhound race live and free
  • Speed maps for all greyhounds
  • Fixed Odds market mover icon
  • Search for you favourite dogs and trainers
  • Your own blackbook to save your favourites so you never miss them running again!
  • Quick form for every runner
  • Full form history and replays for all runners
  • Quick stats for all greyhounds and trainers
  • Integrated live TAB odds and click-to-bet functionality
  • Access to race replays and results for all Victorian greyhound races
  • Race tips, expert analysis and suggested bets
  • Latest greyhound racing news and feature race previews
