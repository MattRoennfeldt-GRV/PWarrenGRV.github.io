Watchdog Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

At GRV, we are committed to producing the best greyhound racing digital experience. Having trouble using some of the features of the Watchdog Application or desktop site? See below for a list of Frequently Asked Questions.

What is "Blackbook"?

Blackbook allows you to follow your favourite dogs AND trainers and receive a daily email summary of when your favourites are scheduled to run the next day. You can also configure Push Notifications to receive an alert when your favourites are about to run.

Where is my Blackbook?

Your Blackbook is accessed from the main menu in the Watchdog App or desktop site. You must have a Watchdog account and be logged in to access your Blackbook.

How do I create a Watchdog account?

You can create a Watchdog account using either an email address and password, or you can login using Facebook, Twitter or a Google account. To create an account, tap or click on the profile button in the top right hand corner of the Watchdog App or desktop site.

Tip: Make sure you verify your account or you will not be able to receive daily email summaries of when our favourite dogs and trainers are scheduled to run.

Need help creating your account or logging in? Check out our Watchdog Account Creation Guide [PDF].

How do I verify my account?

If you sign up using Facebook, Twitter or a Google account, your email address will already be verified, otherwise you will need to check your email Inbox for an email from us requesting you verify your account.

Need help creating your account or logging in? Check out our Watchdog Account Creation Guide [PDF].

Does my FastTrack Account need to be the same as my Watchdog Account?

No. As long as you know your FastTrack login details (Member number or email address used to sign up and current password) you will be able to import your saved favourites directly into Watchdog. Need help creating your account or logging in? Check out our Watchdog Account Creation Guide [PDF].

How is Blackbook different from FastTrackâ€™s eDog Tracker?

The key differences are:

  • Blackbook allows you to follow your favourite Trainers as well as Dogs.
  • Blackbook will send you a daily email the night beforehand showing all your scheduled favourite runners for the next day.
  • Blackbook can send you push notifications for when your favourite runners are about to run.
As a FastTrack eDog Tracker user, do I have to find all my favourite Dogs again?

No. Watchdog Blackbook will allow you to import your saved favourites from FastTrack eDog Tracker. This can be done from your Profile page, accessed through the profile button in the top right hand corner of the Watchdog App or desktop site. For more information on importing your saved dogs, check out our Watchdog Import Guide [PDF].

How do I import my tracked dogs from FastTrack eDog Tracker?

You can import from your account profile page in Watchdog, which will ask for you to enter your FastTrack login information - either your FastTrack Member Number or the email you used to create your account with FastTrack and your FastTrack password.

To begin, simply open your profile page in Watchdog and tap on the Start button in the Import section of the page:

Still need help? Check out our Watchdog Import Guide [PDF].

I already have dogs in my Blackbook, can I still import from FastTrack?

Yes. The import will only import dogs you were following in eDog tracker that are not already in your Blackbook.
