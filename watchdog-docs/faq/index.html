Watchdog Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

At GRV, we are committed to producing the best greyhound racing digital experience. Having trouble using some of the features of the Watchdog Application or desktop site? see below for a list of frequently asked questions.

Blackbook allows you to follow your favourite dogs AND trainers and receive a daily email summary of when your favourites are scheduled to run the next day. You can also configure Push Notifications to receive an alert when your favourites are about to run.

Your Blackbook is accessed from the main menu in the Watchdog App or desktop site. You must be logged in to access your Blackbook.

The key differences are:

  • Blackbook allows you to follow your favourite Trainers as well as Dogs.
  • Blackbook will send you a daily email the night beforehand showing all your scheduled favourite runners for the next day.
  • Blackbook can send you push notifications for when your favourite runners are about to run.
