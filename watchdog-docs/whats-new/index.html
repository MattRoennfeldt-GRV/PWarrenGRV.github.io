GRV’s Watchdog ultimate mobile form guide to greyhound racing in Victoria is now even better!

Now you can create your own account for the Watchdog, which gives you access to a personalised Blackbook and the ability to configure the notifications you want the way you want to receive them.

It’s still your ultimate guide to Victorian greyhound racing and is available on your smartphone or mobile device. Other new features include personalised account and notification settings and your own Blackbook.

The Watchdog mobile form guide is an essential tool for all greyhound racing fans, and it’s still free.

New Watchdog Blackbook Features:

  • Search for your favourite dogs and trainers
  • Bookmark your favourites in your new personal Blackbook
  • Add your own form comments to your Blackbook
  • Quick access to all upcoming and recent favourites
  • Daily email reminders when your favourites are scheduled to run
  • Push Notifications on your phone and Smart Watch so you never miss your favourite Runners again!
